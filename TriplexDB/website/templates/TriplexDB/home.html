Welcome to TripLexicon, the database of RNA:DNA:DNA triple helix formation powered by TriplexAligner!

TripLexicon is a database holding predicted gene regulatory RNA:DNA:DNA triplexes. The triplexes were predicted using TriplexAligner. In its current form, TripLexicon holds predicted triplexes between human lncRNA sequences (as annotated in GENCODE v45/Ensembl release 112) and human gene regulatory elements consisting of promoters (TSS -2500/+500) and regulatory elements from EpiRegio.

Explore the triplexicon queries

  • RNA Query: explore predicted triplexes by RNA gene or transcripts (docs)
  • Target Query: explore predicted triplexes by DNA target genes ( docs)
  • Region Query: explore predicted triplexes by genomic regions in which DNA target elements reside (docs)
